Frequently Asked Questions

Get quick answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary depending on complexity and scope. Simple websites typically take 2-4 weeks, mobile apps 2-6 months, and complex enterprise solutions 6-12 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your development process?

Our comprehensive process includes discovery & planning, UI/UX design, development, testing, deployment, and ongoing support. We provide regular updates, involve you in key decisions, and ensure quality at every step.

Do you provide ongoing support after launch?

Yes! We offer various support packages including bug fixes, security updates, feature enhancements, and technical support. Our goal is to ensure your digital solution continues to perform optimally.

Can you work with existing systems?

Absolutely! We specialize in integrating with existing systems, APIs, and databases. Whether you need to modernize legacy systems or build new features, we'll ensure seamless integration.

What technologies do you specialize in?

We work with cutting-edge technologies including React, Vue.js, Node.js, Python, Unity, blockchain platforms, AR/VR frameworks, and cloud services like AWS and Azure. We choose the best technology stack for each project.

How do you ensure project quality?

Quality is our priority. We follow industry best practices, conduct thorough testing (unit, integration, and user testing), perform code reviews, and maintain comprehensive documentation. We also provide staging environments for your review.
